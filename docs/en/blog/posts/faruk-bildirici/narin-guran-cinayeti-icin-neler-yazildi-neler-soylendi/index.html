What Was Written, What Was Said About the Narin Güran Murder?

I have compiled the headlines and summaries of news reports and commentary published in the media during the roughly 1.5-month period from Narin Güran’s disappearance on 21 August 2024 to the opening days of the trial. In doing so, I prepared a roadmap for myself to examine the journalistic errors related to the Narin murder.

Additionally, for those who have read my analysis titled “12 media errors that influenced the judiciary in the Narin murder”, the following timeline emerged so that they can see the atmosphere that was created in the media during those days:

TIMELINE OF NARIN COVERAGE (21 August – 7 November 2024)

21 AUGUST

  • Narin went to her Quran course after having lunch. However, she did not return home after the course. Footage appeared[1] on the primary school’s camera at 15:15 (TV100).

22 AUGUST

  • Gendarmerie, AFAD and medical teams launched a search in the village. A single slipper, claimed to belong to Narin, was found 3 kilometres from the village.

24 AUGUST

  • Mobilisation for Narin / Mother Yüksel Güran: The slipper found is not Narin’s (Akşam / Sabah / ATV / Haber Global[2]).

25 AUGUST

  • She vanished on the hilltop road / Narin’s last image emerged / A 100-person special team is searching[3] (Akşam / Sabah / Sözcü / DHA / Star TV).

26 AUGUST

  • Blood on the rock does not belong to Narin / False tip-offs infuriated searchers (Akşam).
  • Narin’s mother Yüksel Güran appeared on Didem Aslan Yılmaz’s programme (Show TV).

27 AUGUST

  • Blood trace belongs to the cousin (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Narin’s father protested against false tip-offs[4] (TGRT Haber).

28 AUGUST

  • Gendarmerie Major General Selçuk Yıldırım issued a statement[5] saying “The circle is closing, Narin could be found at any moment” (Medyascope).

29 AUGUST

  • Elder brother Enes was detained due to a bite mark on his arm. However, the owner of the teeth marks could not be determined[6] (Hürriyet / Sabah / Enson Haber / Halk TV).
  • Diyarbakır 5th Magistrate’s Court imposed a media blackout[7] (A Haber).

30 AUGUST

  • It was assessed that Enes having showered three times that day may have contributed to the inability to identify the owner of the teeth marks on his arm (Sabah).

31 AUGUST

  • Uncle Salim Güran, in whose vehicle Narin’s DNA sample was found, was detained[8] (Halk TV).
  • Narin’s elder brother released / Cross-examination of family members (Akşam / Sabah / Sözcü TV).

1 SEPTEMBER

  • New details emerged from the uncle’s phone / Why did the uncle delete[9] his phone records? (Halk TV[10] / Sabah)

2 SEPTEMBER

  • The hashtag #NarinNerede became one of the most shared hashtags in Türkiye on X.
  • Teams searching for Narin found clothing in a cornfield / The entire village is installing cameras / Narin’s uncle is being questioned by a special team (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Narin’s uncle Salim Güran was arrested on charges of premeditated murder (Haber Global[11]).
  • Latest findings in the search for Narin / Retired detective assessed the case (NTV[12]).

3 SEPTEMBER

  • Uncle arrested for ‘premeditated murder’ / It had emerged that the uncle’s phone was off for “1 hour” (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Justice Minister Tunç spoke about the uncle for the first time (Haber Global[13]).

4 SEPTEMBER

  • Search operations were conducted in the cemetery with underground imaging equipment under gendarmerie escort (Hürriyet / Haber Global[14]).
  • Narin’s DNA found on the driver’s seat of the uncle’s vehicle / Uncle could not recall the 2-hour-40-minute gap (Akşam).
  • Shocking claim from a petrol station employee! Was she lying unconscious in her uncle’s vehicle? (A Haber / Gerçek Gündem / Takvim / CNN Türk / Karar / T24 / Türkiye)
  • Striking development in the Narin case! Were the uncle’s phone records accessed[15]? (Halk TV)

5 SEPTEMBER

  • Vomit, urine and blood traces in the uncle’s vehicle belong to Narin (Akşam).
  • Cemetery searched with underground imaging equipment (NTV[16] / Sabah).
  • The mystery of 18:47 in the Narin Güran case (ATV[17] Main News 5 September 2024).

6 SEPTEMBER

  • Narin’s friends are receiving psychological support (Akşam / Sabah / Tele1[18]).

7 SEPTEMBER

  • A new tip-off came in; search operations intensified[19] at Eğertutmaz Creek (NTV / AA / Sözcü TV).

8 SEPTEMBER

  • Missing Narin’s lifeless body was found in the creek after 19 days / 24 people, including her mother, father and sibling, were detained (NTV).
  • AKP Diyarbakır MP Galip Ensarioğlu: “There are things we sometimes do not know, and sometimes know but must not say. Because the family are also our friends.” (KRT TV[20])
  • Did she witness things she should not have seen involving her uncle? (TGRT[21])
  • Expert analysed the footage: She was first strangled, then stabbed (Haber Global[22]).

9 SEPTEMBER

  • Who killed her, who kept silent? 23 people, including family members, are in custody. Now the task is to identify those who committed the murder and those who knew and stayed silent. Is the uncle the killer? (Hürriyet)
  • Was her disabled sibling also killed? / A secret witness gave testimony about those involved in the murder (Akşam / Sözcü[23] / Halk TV[24]).
  • The secret of the murder lies in messages: The prosecutor’s office is pursuing the messages deleted by jailed uncle Salim Güran. (Sabah)
  • The Hizbullah riddle in Narin’s village (Sözcü TV). Claim that Narin’s family is connected to Hizbullah (Tele1).
  • Narin protest in Diyarbakır: “May the hands that touched Narin be broken”, “Killer Hizbullah, collaborator AKP” (Bianet)
  • Media blackout lifted (Halk TV).
  • Fatih Portakal: “A dark family. From the governor to the gendarmerie, from Erdoğan to the Justice Minister, from the Interior Minister to Galip Ensarioğlu — everyone knew the killer. The sister’s departure from life is also full of question marks” (Sözcü TV[25]).
  • İsmail Saymaz: “There are inconsistencies in Nevzat Bahtiyar’s statement…” (Halk TV[26]).

10 SEPTEMBER

  • Take this corpse and dispose of it / The first confession came from plasterer Nevzat B. (Hürriyet / Akşam / Medyascope / Habertürk[27] / Sözcü TV[28]).
  • Gendarmerie is pursuing the second person in the vehicle (Sabah).
  • Nazlı Çelik: “Everyone kept silent, everyone turned a blind eye…” (Star TV)
  • Unbelievable developments in Narin’s death: Her uncle may turn out to be her father (Sözcü TV[29]).
  • Was Narin’s death a collective murder? Were immoral relationships the cause? (CNN Türk[30])
  • Blood-chilling detail about Narin’s death! (Sözcü TV[31]).
  • Retired police officer said “The uncle is experienced” (Sözcü TV[32]).

11 SEPTEMBER

  • Which one is the killer: the brother or the uncle? / 4 scenarios in the Narin murder (Hürriyet)
  • According to senior officials, Narin may have been killed because she witnessed a scene in their own home (Hande Fırat).
  • Her sister’s file was reopened: Tülin Güran’s suspicious death is also being investigated (Akşam / Halk TV[33] / CNN Türk[34]).
  • Uncle Salim is Narin’s killer: Informant Nevzat Bahtiyar stated in his new testimony that the young girl was murdered by uncle Salim Güran (Sabah).
  • The 25-minute mystery in the Narin murder: Whatever happened, it happened in this red car (Haber Global[35]).
  • Breaking development: The ‘3 stones’ detail in the Narin murder! The informant contradicted himself (Sözcü[36]).

12 SEPTEMBER

  • Where does Nevzat fit in? 5 contradictions emerged across Nevzat Bahtiyar’s three separate statements (Hürriyet).
  • Was the jailed uncle’s 353-second phone call a diversion? (Akşam).
  • Illicit affair confession (Sabah[37] / Haber Global[38]).
  • Green headscarf detail in the Narin murder (Haber Global[39]).

13 SEPTEMBER

  • Tavşantepe’s collaborators: Villagers who wanted to misdirect the investigation filed false tip-offs saying ‘The body is here’ for 19 days (Hürriyet).
  • The mother’s contradictory statement (A. Selvi).
  • Mother Yüksel Güran and elder brother Enes Güran were arrested on murder charges (Hürriyet / Sabah / Halk TV[40]).
  • Uncle made his niece give a false statement / ‘Say you saw her at 17:40’ (Akşam).
  • The recording that will solve the murder on Halk TV / Uncle Salim asks his worker: “Did Narin die or is she alive?” (Ferit Demir / Halk TV)
  • Villagers hurriedly installed cameras at their homes (Hürriyet / F. Kızılkoyun).
  • Most homes in the village had security cameras installed while the search for Narin was underway (Now TV / A Haber[41]).

14 SEPTEMBER

  • The devil’s triangle: The entire family is involved (Hürriyet).
  • Salim’s conversation with his worker was deciphered: “She hasn’t died yet” (Akşam / Sözcü TV[42]).
  • “‘I told him to tell everything,’ he did not listen”: Jailed uncle’s wife Melek Güran exposed the lies (Sabah).
  • Communications expert Hüseyin Kazan analysed body language (A Haber[43] / Takvim).

15 SEPTEMBER

  • Two chilling words: “She hasn’t died yet!” (Hürriyet / Haber Global[44]).
  • Audio recordings will crack the secret (Akşam).
  • Following uncle Salim Güran, Nevzat Bahtiyar, mother Yüksel and elder brother Enes were also arrested (Halk TV[45]).
  • Many scenarios, no confession (Sabah).
  • What kind of conscience is this! The family filed false tip-offs to prevent Narin from being found (Tele1[46]).
  • Forensic medicine expert said “Organised” (Sözcü TV[47] / Tele1[48]).

16 SEPTEMBER

  • They even started a fire (Hürriyet).
  • They threw investigators off track with 50 fake tip-offs! (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Did elder brother Enes Güran kill Narin? (Sabah).
  • Beyond the devil’s imagination (Sözcü TV[49]).
  • They used 6 different methods to keep Narin’s body hidden (Tele1[50]).
  • Red slippers were placed at every spot in the village to prevent Narin from being found (Sözcü TV[51]).

17 SEPTEMBER

  • Planning at home, murder in the barn (Hürriyet).
  • The sister-in-law revealed the barn secret (Akşam / Haber Global / Yeni Akit / Star).
  • What did uncle Salim and sister-in-law Hediye discuss? (Sabah).
  • Planning at home, murder in the barn! (Tele1[52] / CNN Türk[53]).
  • Claim that Narin’s decomposition fluid was found on the uncle’s hands (NTV[54]).

18 SEPTEMBER

  • Strangulation evidence in the car (Akşam / Sözcü TV[55]).
  • Arrested for murder, played dumb (Sabah).
  • Twisted relationships in the dark village (Show TV).
  • Narin case solved! Fatih Portakal (Sözcü TV[56]).

19 SEPTEMBER

  • We asked forensic medicine experts: Does the decomposition fluid mean ‘The uncle is the killer’? (Hürriyet).
  • Investigation opened against Tavşantepe’s imam (Akşam).
  • It emerged that Nevzat Bahtiyar spent 18 minutes with Narin’s body at a construction site (Sabah).
  • Forensic medicine expert revealed: Narin’s teeth may have been pulled out one by one! (Gazete Pencere / Sözcü TV[57]).

20 SEPTEMBER

  • Forensic Medicine Institute report: They covered her mouth and nose and suffocated her (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Blood-chilling detail in the murder (Haber Global[58]).

21 SEPTEMBER

  • They silenced the children too (Akşam / Habertürk[59]).

22 SEPTEMBER

  • Uncle killed her, mother watched: Informant Nevzat changed his statement for the 3rd time (Akşam / Sabah / Medyascope / ATV / Now TV / Milliyet).
  • New confession from Nevzat Bahtiyar (Sözcü TV[60]).

23 SEPTEMBER

  • DNA traces were searched for in the informant’s barn (Akşam / Sabah).
  • Justice Minister Tunç: There are certain DNA findings (CNN Türk[61]).
  • DNA is being sought in two cigarette butts found in the barn (Sözcü TV[62]).

24 SEPTEMBER

  • Forensic Medicine: “The owner of the bite mark on Enes’s arm could not be identified” (Akşam / Sözcü TV[63]).

26 SEPTEMBER

  • Yüksel Güran washed the laundry and carpets and bathed twice on the day her daughter disappeared (Süperhaber).

27 SEPTEMBER

  • Narin’s sister-in-law Hediye Güran was arrested on charges of incitement to premeditated murder (Hürriyet).
  • The secrets of Narin’s mother Yüksel Güran and sister-in-law Hediye Güran were revealed (Sözcü TV[64]).

28 SEPTEMBER

  • The mystery two figures on camera (Akşam / Sabah / A Haber[65]).

29 SEPTEMBER

  • One week of footage missing from the camera at Narin’s uncle Hüseyin Güran’s house (Akşam / CNN Türk[66] / Halk TV[67] / Sözcü).

30 SEPTEMBER

  • TÜBİTAK is examining[68] the “Eagle Eye” camera recordings (Sabah / A Haber).

1 OCTOBER

  • The autopsy also yielded no results (Akşam).
  • Father Arif Güran: “It pains me that Nevzat is called an informant” (Sabah).
  • Day 41 of the Narin murder case (Now TV[69]).

2 OCTOBER

  • Narin’s family is seeking compensation / Nevzat carried[70] the body 150 metres in his arms (Akşam / TV100).

7 OCTOBER

  • It was confirmed that Narin was killed at home (Yeni Şafak / A Haber / İnternet Haber).
  • Ferit Demir brought the footage to light (Halk TV / Cumhuriyet / Karar).

14 OCTOBER

  • Presidential Chief Adviser Saadet Oruç challenged the Güran family (YouTube[71]).

23 OCTOBER

  • The completed indictment was submitted to the court (Show TV / Halk TV[72]).

26 OCTOBER

  • New footage of Narin, murdered amid horrifying secrets and twisted relationships, emerged (Show TV / Haber Global[73]).

29 OCTOBER

  • During the search for Narin Güran, the gendarmerie visited a faith healer (DW Türkçe[74] / Rudaw / A Haber / Halk TV[75]).

31 OCTOBER

  • Salim Güran went to the creek bed on the night of the day Narin disappeared (Artı Gerçek / Evrensel / Halk TV / Karar / Kısa Dalga / Show TV).
  • Yet another lie by uncle Salim Güran was debunked! (Haber Global[76]).
  • Bombshell footage: Uncle Salim Güran went to the creek bank that night (Haber Global[77]).

7 NOVEMBER

  • All eyes were fixed, all breath was held. The collaborators put on a show in the courtroom (Show TV[78]).
  • Who answered what in the Narin Güran trial? (Halk TV[79]).
  • Perpetrators in the Narin murder still unknown[80] (Habertürk).
